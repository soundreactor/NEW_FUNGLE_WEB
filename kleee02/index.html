Kleee02

kleee02 is an art piece by Kelian Maissen & Johannes Gees. It is a contemporary, blockchain-inspired take on conceptual art or conceptualism. kleee02 is described in the kleee no 2 smart contract. You can participate by buying your own, unique kleee02 token.(360 of 360 created.) less information ..     [View Gallery on OpenSea.io]

How kleee tokens are created
By sending the specified amount of Ether to the kleee02 smart contract, the token is created and transferred to you wallet (account).

How visualizations are created
The transaction hash from the token creation is used as the variable in the kleee02 algorithm to generate a unique animation, specified by a set of rules that are part of the kleee02 smart contract. Due to the unique variable (hash), each animation is unique.

By following the set of rules described in the smart contract, other artists and coders are able to create other representations of the art piece (material or non-material). Over time, more visualizations of the kleee02 tokens will be made available, such as the original animation as described in the smart contract, digital prints, laser projections and videos.

Properties of the kleee02 tokens
Number of tokens (edition): 360
Token Price: the token price is defined by an algorithm: price = current price +current price/75. The sooner you create a token, the lower the price!

Other ways to participate
kleee02 is open source. Artists, coders, musicians or dancers are free to develop their own version of kleee02, whether it is a projection, an object or a performance.
